Originally from Atlanta, Georgia, Carroll graduated with a degree in Film Studies from The University of North Carolina at Wilmington. Following graduation, she came to Los Angeles to pursue a career in film, where she joined the development team at Madriver Pictures. There she aided in the development of RUIN, starring Margot Robbie and Matthias Schonearts; WHAT IS LIFE WORTH, starring Michael Keaton and Stanley Tucci; and KING OF THE JUNGLE, starring Seth Rogen and Michael Keaton. Outside of her work at Madriver, Carroll has produced music videos, commercial pieces, and fashion campaigns.
Paige Mobley is an American actress, TV personality, Model, and Entrepreneur. She was recently a finalist on the newly revamped America’s Next Top Model on VH1 and MTV. Mobley was born and raised in Birmingham, Michigan and went on to major in Musical Theatre at Pace University in New York City, and now currently resides in Los Angeles. You may have seen Mobley in HBO’s “Here and Now”, CW’s Gossip Girl” and Netflix’s “The Kominsky Method”.
